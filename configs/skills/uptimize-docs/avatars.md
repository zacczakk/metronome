## UPTIMIZE Avatars

UPTIMIZE Avatars, powered by Synthesia, is a leading AI-powered video creation platform that enables users to produce professional quality videos without the need for cameras, microphones, or actors. By converting text into engaging videos featuring realistic AI avatars, UPTIMIZE Avatars simplifies and accelerates the video production process

## Accessing UPTIMIZE Avatars

To receive access to UPTIMIZE Avatars, please complete this [user access request form](https://forms.office.com/Pages/ResponsePage.aspx?id=Wft223ejIEG8VFnerX05yePpQSM-cItJidcmrZ8IGTpUMUlTOFM4WjlESkk3R0xVMEFCTzFCUVgxRi4u)  Your request will be reviewed by your sector admin and if approved, access will be granted within 7 business days.  Please ensure the justification question is filled out so the admins know why you are requesting access.

To be granted access, you will need to review UPTIMIZE Avatars' [terms of use](https://evarooms.merckgroup.com/Topic/MerckData/Files_Services/Synthesia%20at%20Merck%20Terms%20of%20Use%20-%202025%2004%2025.pdf) and [FAQ](https://evarooms.merckgroup.com/Topic/MerckData/Files_Services/Synthesia%20at%20Merck%20-%20User%20Awareness%20and%20FAQs.pdf) documents.


## Getting Started

Once your UPTIMIZE Avatars access has been granted, you will receive a welcome email generated from Synthesia with links to the workspace and tutorials on how to get started.

Note that when you log into Synthesia for the first time you will be prompted through a few questions about how you plan to use the tool.  These questions do not impact the overall performance of UPTIMIZE Avatars, but are mandatory questions from Synthesia to understand how the tool is used.

For information on how to use UPTIMIZE Avatars, we encourage you to consult Synthesia's [documentation](https://docs.synthesia.io/) and [academy](https://academy.synthesia.io/) as it will be the most thorough and up to date source of information.

That being said, there are several important things to note about Merck's set up.  

1.	If you started using Synthesia before receiving an enterprise account, you may need to adjust the workspace you are logged into.  When you are logged into UPTIMIZE Avatars, check what workspace you are working in by looking at the top left corner (to see more options click the down arrow).  Make sure you are working in the relevant sector's workspace.  Note that what workspace options you see may be different than what you see in the screenshot below.


<img src="https://docs.uptimize.merckgroup.com/docs-assets/avatars/workspaces.png" width=350 > 

2.	When working in UPTIMIZE Avatars, there are two types of spaces you can save your videos.  Videos can be saved in the "My videos" section.  This section is only visible to you, and all videos saved here are private.  Alternatively, you can save videos in your sector Workspace.  Videos that are saved in the sector workspace are visible to everyone else in the workspace. This is useful in case multiple folks are working on the same video or in case the maintenance of a video needs to be handed over to someone else in the future.  Generally, we encourage the use of the communal Workspace so that videos are not lost when someone changes roles or leaves the company.

3.	There is some Merck branded material available within UPTIMIZE Avatars.  To find a Merck branded video template, select "New video" then "Workspace" in the left panel.

<img src="https://docs.uptimize.merckgroup.com/docs-assets/avatars/templates.png" width=500 >


Additionally, Merck branded artifacts (such as logos and images) can be found in the Media section when you are creating a video

<img src="https://docs.uptimize.merckgroup.com/docs-assets/avatars/artifacts.png" width=650>

Note that the templates and artifacts available to you may differ by sector workspace.

4.	We generally recommend that when you create a video in UPTIMIZE Avatars that you utilize one of the stock avatars that is already embedded in the system.  However, there are times when it is more impactful to have an avatar of yourself.  To learn more about Merck's guidelines for personal avatars, please review [this document](https://evarooms.merckgroup.com/Topic/MerckData/Files_Services/20250122_guidance%20for%20personal%20avatar.pdf).  Note that personal avatars can only be configured you and they can only be used by yourself or your approved delegates.
