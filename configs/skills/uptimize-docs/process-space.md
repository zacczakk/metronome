# Process Space Capabilties

UPTIMIZE Process Space offers a wide range of capabilities. This is a list of available ones at Merck:


## Process Modeling 

Process modeling is the practice of creating a visual representation of a process to understand, analyze, and improve it. Think of it like drawing a map of how a particular task or workflow is carried out step by step. This can include identifying the inputs, outputs, actions, and decisions involved in the process. By modeling a process, organizations can see where things might go wrong, find ways to make it more efficient, and communicate the process clearly to everyone involved. It's a helpful tool for anyone looking to streamline operations or improve productivity.

## Process Mining

Process mining is a technique used to analyze and improve business processes by extracting knowledge from event logs generated by information systems. Imagine it like digging through a treasure chest of data to find out how work really gets done in a company. By looking at the actual steps taken, how long each step takes, and where bottlenecks occur, organizations can gain insights into their processes. This helps them identify inefficiencies, understand variations, and make data-driven decisions to enhance their workflows. Essentially, process mining helps businesses see the real picture of their operations and find ways to make them better.

## Process Governance

Process governance refers to the framework and set of practices that ensure processes within an organization are managed effectively and aligned with its goals. Think of it as the rules and guidelines that help everyone understand how work should be done, who is responsible for each part of the process, and how to measure success. It involves setting standards, monitoring performance, and making sure that processes are followed consistently. By implementing process governance, organizations can improve accountability, reduce risks, and ensure that their processes are efficient and compliant with regulations. Essentially, it helps keep everything organized and running smoothly.

# Process Manager / Editor

![Process Manager / Editor](https://docs.uptimize.merckgroup.com/docs-assets/process-space/process_manager.png)

SAP Signavio's Process Manager module is a powerful tool designed to help organizations create, visualize, and manage their business processes. Think of it as a digital workspace where you can map out how work gets done, making it easier to understand and improve those processes.

With the Process Manager, users can design process diagrams using simple drag-and-drop features, allowing them to create clear visual representations of workflows. Additionally, the module supports collaboration by allowing team members to comment on and suggest changes to processes in real time. This ensures that everyone can contribute to process improvements and that the most current versions are always available. Overall, the Process Manager module is essential for organizations looking to streamline their operations, enhance communication, and drive continuous improvement.

# Compliance Checker

![Compliance Checker](https://docs.uptimize.merckgroup.com/docs-assets/process-space/compliance_checker.png)

SAP Signavio's Compliance Checker is a tool that helps organizations ensure their business processes are organized and follow established standards (e.g. BPMN 2.0 and process language). Think of it as a quality control system that checks if the way processes are categorized and labeled aligns with the company's guidelines or regulations.

When teams create or update process models, the Compliance Checker reviews them to see if they meet the required criteria. It looks for things like consistent naming conventions, proper classifications, and adherence to specific rules. If any issues are found, the tool provides feedback, helping users correct them before finalizing their work. This ensures that all processes are not only easy to find and understand but also compliant with the organization's standards, leading to better management and communication across teams.

# Governance Workflows

![Governance Workflows](https://docs.uptimize.merckgroup.com/docs-assets/process-space/governance_workflow.png)

SAP Signavio's Governance Workflows are structured processes that help organizations manage and oversee their business operations effectively. Think of them as step-by-step guides that outline how decisions are made, and actions are taken regarding process management.

These workflows ensure that everyone knows their roles and responsibilities when it comes to reviewing, approving, or updating processes. They help maintain consistency and compliance by defining clear paths for how changes should be proposed, evaluated, and implemented. For example, if a team wants to change a process, the Governance Workflow will dictate how that change is submitted, who needs to review it, and how feedback is gathered.

By using Governance Workflows, organizations can improve accountability, reduce risks, and ensure that all process-related changes are made thoughtfully and in line with company policies. This leads to better decision-making and a more organized approach to managing business processes.

# Quick Editor

SAP Signavio's Quick Editor is a simple and intuitive tool that allows users to easily create and modify business process models. Think of it as a user-friendly text editor, but instead of writing documents, you're designing workflows and processes.

With the Quick Editor, users can quickly drag and drop elements like tasks, decisions, and connectors onto a canvas to build their process diagrams. It's designed for speed and ease of use, so even those who may not have a lot of technical expertise can easily make changes or updates to processes. The editor also allows for real-time collaboration, meaning multiple team members can work on the same process model simultaneously, providing input and making edits as needed.

Overall, the Quick Editor makes it fast and straightforward to visualize and refine business processes, helping teams stay agile and responsive to changes in their operations.

# Sharing Your Process Information

With SAP Signavio, you can publish your process diagrams as embedded pictures on web pages, making it easy to share approved processes across the entire organization. This feature allows everyone to access the most current versions of processes directly from their browsers, ensuring that everyone is informed and aligned on how things should be done.

The great advantage of using embedded pictures is that they automatically receive updates whenever a newer version of the same process is created. So, if any changes are made to a process, the embedded picture on the web page will reflect those updates without needing to manually replace or re-upload anything. This ensures that all employees are always looking at the latest, approved processes, helping to maintain consistency and efficiency throughout the organization.

SAP Signavio's Process Intelligence module is a tool that helps organizations analyze and understand their business processes by using data. Imagine it as a detective that digs into the details of how work is actually done, rather than just how it's supposed to be done.
