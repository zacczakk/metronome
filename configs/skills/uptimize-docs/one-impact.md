## 1. Introduction
The One Impact Tool is a Merck internal tool designed for Data & Digital Product Owners. It is used to enter and submit "planned" value KPIs (for next year), "revised" value KPIs (as part of regular Forecasts 1, 2, and 3) and "final" value KPIs for each use case product (final number after a year is over). It will simplify the value collection process and improve the quality and trustworthiness of the numbers, as well as make analyzing and reporting the numbers collected easier.

## 4. How to submit products value KPI's in the tool?
1. Once you are in the landing page click on "Input Page" option on the left top.
<br>![Input Page](/assets/one-impact/4.1.jpg)
2. After opening the Input page, select the Product Outcome field and search for your product. 
   > Note: You can only select products (Use Case Outcomes) in this field. If a Use Case does not have a linked product, no options will be available for selection in this field. For more information, see chapter 12 (Adding product outcomes).

4. After filling the details, click on the Submit button which is located at the end of the page.
<br>![Submit product value](/assets/one-impact/4.4.png)
5. You can find all your products in the Planning View where you can update/edit existing products.
<br>![Planning view](/assets/one-impact/4.5.png)

## 5. Timeline for Product forecast submission
- **Forecast Submission Period**
   - F0: 4th Quarter Previous Year
   - F1: 1st Quarter Current Calendar Year
   - F2: 2nd Quarter Current Calendar Year
   - F3: 3rd Quarter Current Calendar Year
   - YE-Closing: 1st Quarter Current Calendar Year

<br>![Example: Life Science Forecast Timelines](/assets/one-impact/5.1.jpg)

When you provide a forecast, you provide it for the whole year within the quarter, incorporating known value updates about the product outcome. This approach enhances transparency, as you now have a clearer overview of the value contribution. YE-Closing represents the actual figures for the previous year. This is why it is recorded in the 1st quarter of the next year, reflecting on the preceding year. FO is the initial forecast for the product outcome for the upcoming year, serving as the baseline projection for the product's value contribution in the next year, hence collected the last quarter of the current year but for the upcoming year.

## 11. Difference between Opportunity Cost Savings and Actual Cost Savings Property Field
- **Actual cost saving**: You could identify the saved costs on a cost center at Merck if you wanted to.
- **Opportunity cost savings**: These are avoided costs - theoretical costs which could have occurred because (e.g.) additional resources had to be hired, or additional products would have been manufactured, or additional raw materials would have been ordered. You cannot see these cost savings on a cost center because they were actually never raised as additional costs in the first place.

## 12. Delta to previous forecast (new field)
The "Delta to Previous Forecast" field provides the reason for any changes in the forecast value scores compared to the previously submitted forecast to better explain to the Merck organization why numbers have changed within 3 months. 
> For example, the reason for changes in the expected revenue contribution score compared to the previous forecast. Delta: (difference: higher or lower number). 
> **Note**: This field is mandatory and should NEVER be auto-populated with the text from the previous forecast!

## 13. FAQ's
- **What does Financial Impact measure?**  
  Financial metrics that measure impact on financials of all Data Analytics & AI Products at Merck.
  
- **What does Non-Financial Impact measure?**  
  Strategic metrics that measure aspects of a Data Analytics & AI Products business performance that are not directly related to financial outcomes but influence the overall financial outcome.

- **What is a Business Cluster?**  
  Business cluster represents core value streams in a sector. They are a structured grouping of several impact areas. Our core Business clusters are:
  - Research & Development and Innovation
  - Manufacturing
  - Supply chain
  - Sales and Marketing
  - Enablement Clusters

- **What is an Impact area?**  
  An impact area is a specific domain within each business cluster where Key Performance Indicators are measured. (Eg: In Sales and Marketing Business Cluster, we have Additional revenue as an Impact area).

## 14.A For Administrators
### Editing previous Forecast Cycles and modify forecast cycle timeline. 
[Video Tutorial](https://mdigital-my.sharepoint.com/:v:/g/personal/m246776_one_merckgroup_com/EcgsC38mwkhJj4vl_SxtiUsBNKxEJLq-erw-1kwakz59Hw?nav=eyJyZWZlcnJhbEluZm8iOnsicmVmZXJyYWxBcHAiOiJTdHJlYW1XZWJBcHAiLCJyZWZlcnJhbFZpZXciOiJTaGFyZURpYWxvZy1MaW5rIiwicmVmZXJyYWxBcHBQbGF0Zm9ybSI6IldlYiIsInJlZmVycmFsTW9kZSI6InZpZXcifX0%3D&e=bzntE8)
As an admin, you can edit previous forecast cycle information by following the below steps:
1. Go to UPTIMIZE One Impact Project Folder - "global-use-case-one-impact".
<br>![admin edit](/assets/one-impact/14.1.gif)
2. Click on "One Impact Forecast Cycle Year" and the below page opens.
<br>![admin edit](/assets/one-impact/14.2.jpg)
3. Select your sector (by default it should be your sector).
4. Enter which year you want to make changes. (Eg: 2024).
5. Select which forecast cycle you want to make changes. (Eg: F3).
6. Click "Submit".

Now you will be able to update information for all products in your portfolio for the selected year and selected forecast cycle. Once you have made the changes, please revert to the current forecast cycle or else the recently executed changes will remain. Please be aware that all the product owners who have access to the tool will be able to see the changes in forecast cycle. Inform them before making any changes to forecast cycle or else their ongoing progress might be interrupted.

### 14.B Editing Product Submissions
As an admin, you can view all the products and make changes to them if necessary. To do so, follow the below steps:
1. Click on Planning View.
2. You will see all the products for your sector workspace and select the product you would like to make changes.
3. Once the product is selected click on "Admin Edit" option as displayed below and make changes to the product.
<br>![admin edit product](/assets/one-impact/14.3.jpg)
4. Once all changes are made, click submit and exit.
